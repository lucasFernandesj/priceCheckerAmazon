About
The price of products sold online may fluctuate, they may be now costing too much but they might eventually be on a sale and available at an attractive price for the next few hours only to go back to it's original price tomorrow.
With the Amazon Price Chacker© by Fernandes Programming you can enter a product that you want to buy and how much do you want to pay for it and the app will automatically check for it's price and send you a notification once it's price goes bellow what you wanted it to be.
